Introduction

Welcome to MechConnect Shop Owner Platform. We are committed to protecting your business information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice or our practices with regard to your business information, please contact us at privacy@mechconnect.com.

Information We Collect

Business Information

We collect business information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register your shop, manage your services, process bookings, or otherwise interact with our platform.

  • Shop Details - Business name, address, contact information, operating hours
  • Service Information - Services offered, pricing, availability
  • Mechanic Data - Mechanic profiles, certifications, specializations
  • Financial Data - Revenue reports, payment information, transaction history
  • Booking Records - Customer bookings, service history, customer feedback
